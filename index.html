Business intelligence

Heat map-based business intelligence for mapping companies in the UK

View project on GitHub

A joint research with Digital Catapult UK to accelerate technology adoption across UK with focus on small-medium enterprises (SME). The system prototype analyses SME data in the UK by using custom-made data analytics tools made from Python, PHP and JavaScript. Results are shown interactively using Google Maps heat map as a simple, user-friendly approach for decision makers.

This system was created to answer real-life problem that occurs in London, as published on my Linkedin page: https://www.linkedin.com/today/post/article/mapping-business-foresee-potential-areas-development-alam-widigya?trk=prof-post
