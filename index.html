Business intelligence System Based on Companies' Data in the UK

Heat map-based business intelligence for mapping companies in the UK

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

A joint research with Digital Catapult UK to accelerate technology adoption across UK with focus on small-medium enterprises (SME). The system prototype analyses SME data in the UK by using custom-made data analytics tools made from Python, PHP and JavaScript. Results are shown interactively using Google Maps heat map as a simple, user-friendly approach for decision makers. This system was created to answer real-life problem that occurs in London, as published on my Linkedin page.
